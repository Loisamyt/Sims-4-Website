How to download Sims 4 cc

Hi! this is a helpful guide on how to safely and quickly install CC to your Sims 4 game:) What is CC (custom content)? Many players of The Sims 4 choose to enhance their game with custom content. Created by fans, custom content, or CC as it's often known, is a free way to add new clothing, hairstyles, furniture, and more to your game. *note, you need to have The Sims 4 game downloaded already*

  1. Visit this website to find the perfect CC (custom content) for your game.
  2. Press the download button and wait for the website to tell you that it has succesfully been downloaded.
  3. Open your file explorer and go to your ‘downloads’ folder.
  4. Select the CC that you recently downloaded and drag it into ‘This pc > documents > Electronic arts > The Sims 4 > Mods’. *note, if it is a zip/package file you need to ‘export here’*
  5. Open your Sims game, go to ‘Game Options’, then ‘Other’. From there, you can select ‘Enable custom content and mods’ , then press the ‘apply changes’ button.
  6. Restart (exit/end game then go back in) your Sims game and the CC (custom content) should be in there.
